Instructions

Your hero runs on their own! press spacebar to jump.(The longer you hold spacebar the higher the jump to a certain point)

and jumping platform to platform, also watch out for the
the tiny scary red scorpions and cactus! Dont worry about the shrubs they wont hurt you.
Also the further you go the faster your hero will run!
